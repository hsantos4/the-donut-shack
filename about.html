This journey started over 10 years ago. Duane and I went to the Christmas lights at the charlotte motor speedway and saw our first Lil orbits donut machine.


We were so fascinated with this donut machine that made fresh and hot donuts, that we stood there for 20 minutes watching the donuts come off the conveyor belt.


We saw how people enjoyed the taste of these mini donuts, and we looked at each other and said we need to get one of those.


At that time, we had 4 kids at home, and the money just was not there so we put our dreams on hold.


Over the years, out of the blue one of us would say something about that Lil orbits donut machine and how it would be nice to have one, but we never really thought our Dream would come true.


Fast forward to 2021. We are now empty-nesters, and I (Dawn), decided my time in corporate healthcare (28 years) was coming to an end, and I knew I needed a new direction for my life, but what direction would I go?

We have prayed and sought the Lord, and the opportunity finally presented itself for us to open a mini donut concession trailer.


When I tell people how the pieces just came all together, it seems surreal, but there is no doubt that this was all the Lord doing because without HIM none of this is possible.


The next challenge was finding a name. We both came up with names that the other one did not like, and one day while Duane was driving to work, I was trying to figure out a name.


The Donut Shack came into my head, and at that moment Duane called and said I have the perfect name “The Donut Shack” so it came to be and then Duane designed our logo and picked our color scheme - purple is for royalty and red is for the blood of Jesus…


So now, after many years, our journey to bring hot and fresh mini donuts to the Carolinas begins!


We hope to see you soon! Dawn and Duane Hunt
